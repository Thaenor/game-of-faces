WTF is this

Hi, welcome to Jinx, the game of faces. This is a web-app I wrote up in 2 or 3 hours. It started as joke I came up with a friend to play the "game of faces" like Aria in Game of Thrones. If you enjoy this, you can reach me at Twitter. Check out the github page in case you want to contribute.

The rules

It's very simple. First you can set your name as your friend's. Tap the name of the person telling the story and then just hit the button if the other person believed it or if it caught you lying. At the moment points and names aren't saved, but... they might
